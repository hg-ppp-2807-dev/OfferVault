# 🎯 PlaceCoach — AI Placement Coach MVP

A production-ready AI placement coach designed to help users prepare for job placements efficiently.

---

## 🚨 The Problem

Preparing for job placements is often unstructured and overwhelming:
- **Resumes** frequently fail to pass through ATS filters due to formatting issues or missing keywords, without any feedback to the candidate.
- **Interviews** are hard to practice for without realistic, role-specific questions and constructive evaluations.
- **Preparation Planning** lacks direction, leaving candidates unsure of what skills to study and how to pace their learning.

## 💡 The Solution

PlaceCoach provides an AI-driven, comprehensive approach to solve these placement preparation challenges:

- **Resume Analyzer:** Users can upload a PDF to get their resume extracted and scored by AI, instantly revealing critical mistakes and offering actionable, line-by-line improvements.
- **Mock Interview Simulator:** Provides a chat-based mock interview with role-specific questions tailored to the user's target job, followed by an AI evaluation of their communication and technical answers.
- **Roadmap Generator:** Users input their target role and current skills to receive a customized, step-by-step preparation plan, complete with daily tasks and weekly milestones.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js 14
- **AI Engine:** ollama (llama3.2)
- **Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- **Styling & UI:** Tailwind CSS
